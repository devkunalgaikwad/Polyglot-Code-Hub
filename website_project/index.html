Team Abhedya

The Baja team is a group of engineering students who design, build, and race off-road vehicles. They participate in the Baja SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) competitions, where teams from different colleges showcase their vehicles' durability, performance, and design.

Team Abhedya Racing

The Supra team focuses on designing and manufacturing Formula-style race cars. They participate in events like Formula SAE and Formula Student, where teams compete in various dynamic and static challenges, including acceleration, endurance, design evaluation, and business presentation.

Team Achilles

The Efficycle team is dedicated to building human-powered three-wheeled vehicles. These vehicles are designed for efficiency, sustainability, and innovation. They compete in events like the national-level Efficycle competition organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE).

Team Rudra

The Robocon team is involved in designing and developing robots to compete in the prestigious national and international Robocon competitions. These competitions challenge teams to create robots that can complete specific tasks and objectives, showcasing technical expertise, teamwork, and innovative problem-solving skills.

Team NSS

National Service Scheme is a voluntary organization that focuses on community service and social work. The NSS team actively engages in various activities such as cleanliness drives, blood donation camps, awareness campaigns, and rural development programs to contribute to society's well-being.

Team NCC

The National Cadet Corps team provides students with opportunities for character building, leadership development, and national integration. NCC activities include drill training, adventure camps, social service, and participating in national-level parades and events
